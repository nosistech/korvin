# document-drafter

**Trigger:** "Write a [type] about [topic]"

**Action:**
1. Identify the document type (email, report, proposal, blog post, etc.).
2. Ask clarifying questions if needed (tone, length, audience).
3. Generate a draft in the user’s preferred style.
4. Over time, learn the user’s voice from past messages and refine output.

**Example:**  
User: *“Write a polite email to a client about a delayed shipment”*  
→ Produces a ready‑to‑send email with placeholders for names/dates.
